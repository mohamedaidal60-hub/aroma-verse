Agarwood Oil Siam

Agarwood Oil Siam is produced by a distiller and graduate of PerfumersWorld that we have worked with for over 10 years. This specific oil is our favorite and guaranteed natural from plantations close to the Thai-Cambodian border and produced by ethical and sustainable methods. Agarwood Oil Siam has the typical agarwood/oud character with, rich woody balsamic, animalic-phenolic and leather notes, only slightly woody-smoky. It does NOT have the undesirable green-wood notes of trees that have been harvested too young.
