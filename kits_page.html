Perfume RM Kits

PerfumersWorld perfume raw material kits are designed to get you into perfumery quickly and as far down the rabbit hole as you want go, as painlessly as possible. All the kits use fragrance industry premium raw materials so can be blended with your own pure essential oils and aroma chemicals for use in scented products.

The Perfumery Training Kit K26

The Original Perfumery Training Kit that has made learning perfumery as simple as ABC for 25 years is still available for those that prefer "original is best". If you are not sure which to choose then we recommend that the brand-new F-TEC (Fleuressence TEChnology) version is even easier to use.

The F-TEC Perfumery Training Kit

The F-TEC (Fleuressence-TEChnology) Perfumery Training Kit that makes learning perfumery as simple as ABC and forms such a deep understanding of perfumery classifications that it can actually revolutionize the way you perceive ('olfactorize') smells. The F-TEC version has been updated with longer odour lives, better compatibility with oil and waxed based products like Candles, Melts, Massage Oils etc. and easier IFRA compliance.
